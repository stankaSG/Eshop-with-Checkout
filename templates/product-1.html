HairTry - Everyone, Everyday Conditioner for daily use for all hair types from HairTry - softening conditioner, suitable for all hair types. It has fresh and at the same time elegant fragrance.

Among other ingredients, it contains broccoli seed oil, which has a similar effect to silicone, significantly smooths the hair and protects it from external factors influences. It also contains panthenol, which as a moisturizer helps to maintain the correct level moisture and regenerates hair fibers. Hydrolyzed jojoba esters smooth the hair and in addition, they protect dyed hair from color loss. The combination of these ingredients creates a mixture ideal for everyone, for daily use, which will take care of hydration and shine.

Ingredients: Water, Broccoli oil, Panthenol, Hydrolyzed jojoba esters, Glycerin, Vitamin E, Natural fragrances.

Use: Apply the conditioner to shampooed and damp hair. Comb the preparation with a brush with wide teeth or a comb. Leave on for a few minutes and then rinse with water.

Volume: 250 ml

Expiry date: 6 months after opening.

Country of origin: Poland.
