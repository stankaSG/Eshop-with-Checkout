It intensively smooths, adds shine and exceptional softness. It has a liquid consistency that turns into a light emulsion when applied to damp hair and rubbed, and heat is generated in the process.

The product is based on a large amount of oil ingredients, so it replenishes lipids at a rapid pace, thus very strongly smoothing and conditioning unruly strands. The conditioner makes combing and styling easier, while not weighing hair down. It provides a banquet effect, which will delight owners of dull, rough and tangle-prone hair. Packaging with an applicator will guarantee convenient application. Our conditioner can freeze at minus temperatures (e.g., in transit), which can cause it to change its consistency into an emulsion. Once it hits room temperature, it will return to its liquid form, retaining its properties.

Ingredients: Propylene Glycol, Alcohol Denat., Myristyl Alcohol, Aqua, Polysorbate 20, Behentrimonium Chloride, C9-12 Alkane, Cetrimonium Chloride, C13-15 Alkane, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Parfum, Isopropyl Alcohol

Use: After shampooing, apply to damp hair, avoiding the scalp. Massage for 5-8 seconds until a light emulsion forms, then rinse. Avoid contact with eyes. Accompanying heat during application is normal. Apply once every 7-10 days. Do an allergy test before the first use.

Volume: 100 ml

Expiry date: 6 months after opening.
